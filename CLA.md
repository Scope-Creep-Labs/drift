## How to sign

When You open Your first pull request to a Drift repository, our **CLA Assistant bot** will post a comment on the PR with a one-click link to record Your acceptance of this Agreement. The bot checks the signature on every subsequent PR You open and will block merging if it's missing. Your signature covers all Your future Contributions to all Drift repositories; You do not need to re-sign.

If You prefer to sign manually (e.g., on behalf of an employer that requires a wet signature), email a signed copy of this Agreement to **support@scopecreeplabs.com**. We'll add You to the manual roster the bot consults.
